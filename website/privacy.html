Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

In short: Noteora stores your notes only on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to your notes. This website does not use cookies or analytics of any kind.

1. Who we are

Noteora ("we", "us", "our") develops the Noteora mobile application (the "App") and operates this website (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App and the Site do — and do not — collect, and how that information is handled.

2. Data stored by the App

Noteora is designed to work fully offline. All content you create — note text, handwriting strokes, folders, tags, images you insert, reminders, and PIN locks — is stored exclusively in a local database on your own device. This data is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted to us or to any third party. We do not operate a server that receives, stores, or has access to your notes.

Because your notes never leave your device, we have no ability to view, recover, or delete them on your behalf. If you delete the App or your device, that data is gone unless you have exported or backed it up yourself (for example, via a PDF or text file you saved elsewhere).

3. PIN protection

You can lock individual notes or folders with a PIN. The PIN is stored on your device in a salted, hashed form — not as readable text — and is used only to unlock content locally within the App. We never receive your PIN and cannot reset or recover it for you; an optional hint you create yourself is the only recovery option.

4. No accounts, no cloud sync

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address, name, or any other personal identifier to use it. There is no cloud sync feature, so no note content is ever associated with an identity we hold.

5. Device permissions

The App may request the following device permissions, used solely for the features you actively use:

  • Photo library access — to let you insert an existing image into a note.
  • Camera access — to let you take a photo and insert it into a note.

Images you insert are stored locally alongside your note and are handled the same way as all other note content described in Section 2.

6. Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Noteora does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking libraries. We do not build advertising profiles, and we do not sell or share data, because we do not collect any in the first place.

7. This website and cookies

This website does not use cookies, and it does not load any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts. The only piece of information the Site stores in your browser is your light/dark theme preference, saved using your browser's local storage. That value stays on your device, is never transmitted anywhere, and is used for no purpose other than remembering your display preference on your next visit.

8. App Store data collection

Downloading the App from the App Store involves standard, platform-level information collected by Apple to operate the App Store itself (such as download counts and crash diagnostics, if you opt in to share them with developers). That collection is governed by Apple's own privacy practices, not by us, and is disclosed separately on the App's App Store product page.

9. Children's privacy

Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is not directed at soliciting personal data from users of any age.

10. Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since the App itself collects no data, changes here are expected to be rare and limited to clarifying language.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to info@perfectaistudio.com.
